The Ashes

In 2015 England and Australia will contest the 69th Ashes series, Australia having won 32 of their previous encounters and England 31. Australia enjoyed their greatest run of dominance between 1989 and 2002, when they snaffled eight series on the spin, while England are statistically buoyed by victory in 11 of the first 12 contests, all of them in the 19th century. Over the last 100 years Australia have a less wafer-thin 25-16 lead in Ashes victories, but for all Australia’s sometime superiority the countries’ cricketing rivalry remains red-hot.
